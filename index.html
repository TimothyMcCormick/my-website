About Me

I am a full stack software developer. I enjoy problem-solving and creatively coming up with solutions. I dabbled in coding, starting with Python, while at my previous job as a laser engraver and loved it. I decided to go all in and leave my job for Boise Codeworks. My decision to go to Codeworks was one of the best decisions I have ever made. During my time there I have learned many new languages, frameworks and the foundations for software development. In addition, I learned the values of setting goals and how to navigate through the different steps to achieve those goals and how to work in a team environment utilizing Scrum/Agile Principles. I am seeking an opportunity in the exciting, ever-changing career field of Software Development and looking to expand on my skills and abilities.
I come from a background in manufacturing where I was given the opportunity to teach myself a variety of different skills including CorelDraw, AutoDesk Fusion360, UltiMaker Cura (for 3D Printing), CNC Machining, Plasma Cutting, and Co2 and Fiber Laser Technology. Before this I worked in construction, particularly in renovation, where I learned multiple different trades and was able to put those abilities into practice on my own home remodel. I have always had the desire to learn new things.
Aside from this, I enjoy being outdoors (backpacking, camping), playing drums/guitar, snowboarding, biking, dirtbiking, flying drones (FPV and LOS) and spending time with my amazing wife and four beautiful children.

My Portfolio

Keepr

Keepr is a full stack photo-sharing application built out over a period of a week. Users can add photos (keeps), see other users' photos and add them to collections (vaults). The back-end API is created with C#, MySQL and Dapper as the ORM. For the front-end I utilized Vue.Js, HTML and CSS, as well as a masonry layout for "keeps" cards.

On-tracker

Collaborated on a team of 5 developers practicing Scrum/Agile principles to build out a full stack, project management application using Vue.JS, Node.JS, JavaScript, Mapbox API, MongoDB and Auth0 for user management. Users can add a business, track active projects, add/assign employees to projects, create project tasks and make notes. Utilized the Mapbox API to view pins on home business location and the locations of all your active projects.

Tower

Full stack application designed to get users with common interests together for events. Built with Vue.Js for the front-end and Express, Node.js and MongoDB using Mongoose as the ORM. Implemented Auth0 for account management.
